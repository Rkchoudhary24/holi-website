Origin of Holi

Holi is an ancient Hindu festival celebrated in India, marking the arrival of spring. It symbolizes the victory of good over evil, inspired by the legend of Prahlad and Holika.

How Holi is Celebrated?

People play with colors, sing and dance, and share sweets like Gujiya and Thandai.
